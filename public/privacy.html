Privacy Policy

Tenant Switcher Limited ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we handle it.

1. Information We Collect

Tenant Switcher does not collect personal data directly through the desktop application. Limited information, such as license key, device ID, and activation status, may be stored securely to validate your license.

2. Payment Information

Payments and subscriptions are processed through Stripe. We do not store or have access to your credit card details. Stripe’s privacy policy applies to all transactions (https://stripe.com/privacy).

3. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Cloudflare Insights) to collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the site. No personally identifiable information is stored or sold.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information using encryption and secure storage. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights

If you are a New Zealand resident, you have rights under the Privacy Act 2020 to access and correct your personal information. You may contact us at support@tenant-switcher.com for such requests.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
